About StartupResources.io

StartupResources.io founder, Stuart

I've been doing "Startup stuff" for two years and have always been interested in finding the best tools to use.

Some of these tools have odd names, and I decided to start writing them down after once racking my brain to try to remember a tool that I had previously been told about. Then I decided to better yet create a site that listed all the tools publically. And this is it.

If you know of a tool that is awesome and should be included, please let me know about it. There's a contact form on the bottom of each page. Or just email me about it.
